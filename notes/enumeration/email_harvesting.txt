The Harvester searches for email addresses from public data sources on a given domain.
-b: data source: google, googleCSE, bing, bingapi, pgp, linkedin, google-profiles, jigsaw, twitter, googleplus, all

Examples:
theharvester -d cisco.com -b google >google.txt
theharvester -d cisco.com -l 10 -b bing >bing.txt
